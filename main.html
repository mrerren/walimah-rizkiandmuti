Ta'aruf Steps

  • Exchange our CV

    Our mediators are our ustadz n ustadzah.

    We write anything about ourself detail. So he/she got the description almost perfect.

  • Istikharah (clear your heart and do this because of Allah)

    In this part, ask Allah what's best for us. Don't fall in love to him/her for a start, but love Allah first. So we will surrender to what Allah has determined for us long ago.

  • Set the date for first meeting

    Make sure that your potential mate is your potential type.

    People can never be so sure, but you have to choose as close as your type by being honest to yourself.


    The meeting is to check whether your choice is correct. Usually we will get affirmation about our feeling in this part.

  • Dig up about your potential mate profusely with shari'a rule

    Collect a lot of information about your potential mate, for example how he/she is in his family, how he/she want for this marriage, how he/she react if he get mad, etc.

  • Set the time for family meeting

    To enter this part you have to be sure you really want to marry the one you have chosen. Because this will bring your loving persons such as mom, dad, and whole family to get involved in your marriage plan.

  • Set the time for your Wedding schedule

    This could based on family meeting, or just on woman's parent wish.

All of this procedure are not rigid. We just tell you what we've been through. Every couple are unique, just do the things right so Allah will bless your intention


Just believe for every Allah's plan.

How To Get There?

Beberapa jenis transportasi yang dapat Anda gunakan:

  • Commuter Line

    Turun Stasiun Klender Baru lalu naik angkot 20 (K20) turun di wijaya kusuma 2 jalan kaki sampai ke lokasi.

  • Angkutan Umum

    • Angkot 32 (M32) pilih jurusan perumnas klender turun di wijaya kusuma 2 lalu jalan menuju lokasi.
    • Angkot 03 (K03) pilih jurusan perumnas klender turun di pasar perumnas lalu jalan menuju lokasi.
    • Jak Lingko rawamangun (JAK 34) turun di perumnas klender lalu jalan menuju lokasi.
